About Westover Digital

The Idea for Westover Digital was conceived after our founder, Tim, was working with a number of small businesses as a freelancer who most of them have had some kind of bad experiences with Outsourced Development companies.

Westover Digital is one of the only companies in the United Kingdom who really believe in their clients and believe about notcharging the earth.

We’re a young, energetic and friendly team of misfits who are obsessively passionate about changing the way Website + Software Development is done. We’re relentlessly innovating and we’d love you to join us for the journey.
